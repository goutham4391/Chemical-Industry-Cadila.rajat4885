Straddling the Entire Life Science Value Chain

Our 360-degree business operations cover R&D, API, Formulations, Marketing and Drug Discovery.

Strong API Legacy

We synthesize quality API solutions that span across leading therapeutic categories, namely Respiratory, Diabetology, Gastroenterology, Pain management and Orthopedics.

State-of-the-Art Manufacturing

Our ultra-modern facility conforms to the stringent international norms like USFDA, European Union, MHRA UK, WHO Geneva, TGA Australia, etc.

Robust Research

Our 350 scientists working across our dedicated R&D setups are a testament to our competencies in undertaking research in therapeutic areas with unmet medical needs.

World’s First Innovation

At Cadila, we are proud to be manufacturers of first-in-the-world innovations in the areas of lung cancer, tuberculosis, cardio-vascular diseases and gastroenterology.

A Global Outreach

We are present in all regulated markets of the USA, Europe and Japan and Africa. With strategic collaborations and operations in over 100 countries, Cadila has a multicultural, multilingual and multinational workforce of more than 9000 employees including over two hundred people outside India in Africa, CIS, Japan and USA.

Our Inspiration

Known as the ‘Medicine Man of India’, Cadila´s Founder Chairman, Indravadan A. Modi (1926-2012) was a champion of the Indian Patents Act 1970. A doyen of the Indian drug discovery industry, who rose from the ranks to establish a leading Indian pharmaceuticals company, believed in making modern medicine affordable and accessible to the last man in the society.

The Care Continues...
